# Quick Start

NOTE: This is a placeholder page. Codryn is currently in development and will be available in the future.

Get up and running in under five minutes.

```{tip}
Before continuing, make sure you have completed the {doc}`installation` steps.
```

---

## Step 1 — Create Your Account

Launch Codryn and tap **Sign Up**. Enter your email address and choose a password.
A verification link will be sent to your inbox.

## Step 2 — Create Your First Character

Once logged in, tap the **+** button on the *Characters* tab.

```{mermaid}
sequenceDiagram
    participant User
    participant Codryn

    User->>Codryn: Tap + on Characters tab
    Codryn-->>User: Show character creation wizard
    User->>Codryn: Choose race, class & name
    Codryn-->>User: Roll ability scores
    User->>Codryn: Confirm character sheet
    Codryn-->>User: Character created ✓
```

Fill in the character creation wizard:

1. **Name** — choose wisely, adventurer!
2. **Race** — Human, Elf, Dwarf, and many more.
3. **Class** — Wizard, Fighter, Rogue, Cleric …
4. **Ability Scores** — roll or use standard array.

When you are happy, tap **Create Character** to save.

## Step 3 — Start a Campaign

Switch to the **Campaigns** tab and tap **New Campaign**.
Give your campaign a name and an optional description, then invite your party
members via their Codryn usernames or email addresses.

For a full guide on managing campaigns, see {doc}`../features/campaign-tracking`.

## Step 4 — Roll Some Dice

Tap the 🎲 icon in the bottom navigation bar to open the **Dice Roller**.
Select a die type (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, or d20) and tap **Roll**.

Your results are saved in the *Roll History* panel — perfect for disputed rolls!

More details are available in {doc}`../features/dice-roller`.
